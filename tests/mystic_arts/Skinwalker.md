### Traits
***Legendary Resistance (3/Day, or 4/Day in Lair).*** If the skinwalker fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

***Magic Resistance.*** The skinwalker has [[Advantage]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

***Wear Skin.*** The skinwalker can spend a minute to flawlessly adopt the form of any creature it's killed with Skin Alive. Its statistics remain the same in any form.

### Actions
***Multiattack.*** The skinwalker can make a two attacks, claw and skin alive, in any combination.

***Claw.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 17 (2d10 + 6) piercing damage plus 5 (1d10) necrotic damage and the skinwalker grapples the creature (escape DC 19).

***Skin Alive.*** Constitution Saving Throw: DC 16, one creature that is [[grappled]] by the skinwalker's claws. *Failure:* 45 (10d8) necrotic damage. *Success:* Half damage. *Failure or Success:* If this damage reduces the target to 0 Hit Points, the skinwalker skins the target alive and kills it.

***Shadow Walk.*** The Skinwalker can teleport up to 300 ft to a point it can see in dim light or darkness.

***Spellcasting.*** The Skinwalker casts one of the following spells, requiring no spell components and using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 15):

**At Will:** [[Thaumaturgy]], [[Dissonant Whispers]], [[Detect Thoughts]], [[Darkness]], [[Silence]]
**1/Day Each:**  [[Fear]], [[Confusion]], [[Mislead]]

### Bonus Actions
***Shadow Blend.*** While in dim light or darkness, the cryptid becomes [[invisible]], until the start of its next turn or until the cryptid is in bright light.

***Ill Omen (1/Day)***. Wisdom Saving Throw: DC 15, each creature within a 60-foot [[emanation]] of the cryptid. Failure: The target has the [[frightened]] and repeats the save at the end of its next turn if it is still [[frightened]], ending the effect on itself on a success. If the target failed by 5 or more it's [[stunned]] in addition to being frightened. Success: The target is immune to this gaze for the next 24 hours.

### Reactions
***Ephemeral.*** *Trigger:* The cryptid is takes damage from an attack while in Dim Light or Darkness. *Response:* The cryptid can reduce the damage by 11 (2d10) and teleports to a point it can see within 30 feet.

***Whispers Around (1/Round).*** Wisdom Saving Throw: DC 17, a 20-foot [[emanation]] from point the skinwalker can see within 90 feet. *Failure:* 18 (5d6) psychic damage and must immediately use its reaction, if available, to move towards the skinwalker using the safest route. *Success:* Half damage only.

***Terrorize (1/Round).*** The skinwalker casts an at-will spell with its Spellcasting.

***Lounge.*** The skinwalker moves up its speed, and it makes one claw attack.
